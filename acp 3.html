The History of Food and Nutrition

Throughout history, food has played a crucial role in shaping civilizations, economies, and cultures.
Ancient societies, such as Mesopotamians and Egyptians, developed farming techniques that led to the rise of early civilizations.
The Agricultural Revolution marked a turning point, allowing humans to transition from hunter-gatherers to settled communities.

Trade routes, such as the Silk Road, spread food varieties across continents, influencing diets and culinary traditions.

Modern advancements in nutrition science have provided deeper insights into food composition, dietary needs, and global food security.
